A Figure in Front of a Painting

– 35mm MONO 1:33
1984 by Kathy Smith 45 seconds-color-sound

Based on six mixed media artworks created by the artist in 1984, A Figure in Front of a Painting is simply a rhythmic exploration of image, movement and sound.

Exhibited as an installation and also separately with animation alone. Six large format works exist and 380 individual mixed images on paper created by the artist.
