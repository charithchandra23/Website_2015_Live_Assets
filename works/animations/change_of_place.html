Change of PLACE

– 16mm MONO 1:33


1985 by Kathy Smith 4 minutes-color-sound

Change of PLACE is an allegorical journey into memory where certain events, happenings places and people resonate with the subject. These impressions are felt and expressed in contrasting environments, urban and rural.

Art work created on 16” x 12” animation paper using mixed media, gauche, ink, air brush, charcoal and photographic prints. Approximately 2,000 original images all created by the artist exist for this film
